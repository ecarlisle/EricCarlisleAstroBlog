This may belong in a “yet another” category for 3D modeling, but yes, I wanted to design something for managing the countless portable media and dongles I hang onto. I also wanted a rectangular plot instead of a long strip that I see in many other models. Though this isn’t a remix, this was definitely inspired by a [USB SD and MicroSD holder](https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:2637487) I’ve used until now.

This model can hold:

- 24 USB-A sticks and/or dongles
- 16 SD cards
- 29 MicroSD cards

## Estimated Resources

- **Print Time:** 13hrs, 33min (with 150mm/s print speed)
- **Material Use:** 108g, 36.11m

Sliced with [Ultimaker Cura](https://ultimaker.com/software/ultimaker-cura/).

## Recommended Print Settings

- **Resolution:** 0.2mm
- **Support:** none
- **Infill:** 20%
- **Raft/Brim:** none
- **Vertical Walls:** 4
- **Horizontal Walls:** 3
