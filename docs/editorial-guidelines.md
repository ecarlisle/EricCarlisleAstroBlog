# Editorial Guidelines

The blog should sound like an experienced software developer reflecting clearly on practical
engineering work.

## Preferred Voice

- Grounded
- Clear
- Human-first
- Thoughtful
- Practical
- Curious
- Experienced without sounding inflated

## Avoid

- Generic AI hype
- Generic AI doom
- Empty thought-leadership phrases
- Overly corporate polish
- Dense academic framing
- Clickbait titles

## Good Article Shape

1. Start with a concrete experience, question, or tension.
2. Connect it to a broader software development theme.
3. Explain the practical implication.
4. Give the reader something useful to carry forward.
5. End with a grounded reflection rather than a slogan.
